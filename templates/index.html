About

Born and raised in Liverpool, I have always had a passion for technology and education. After completing my Diploma of Higher Education in the UK, I took advantage of the opportunity to study in Madrid through an Erasmus program. This experience not only allowed me to immerse myself in a new culture, but also sparked my interest in different approaches to education.

As a result, in 2019 I completed my Masters Degree while living here and working as a teacher.

With a desire to find greater satisfaction, I made a career change in the summer of 2022. I had had the opportunity to teach basic coding classes at a school, and this experience solidified my passion for technology and programming.

Since then, I have been continuously learning and developing my skills in Python, HTML, CSS, and other related technologies. I have gained experience with Jinja templating, Bootstrap, Flask, and SQL, among others.

I am eager to start my journey in the tech industry and am now actively seeking my first position. I am a quick learner, highly motivated, and committed to using my skills and knowledge to make a difference.

Feel free to reach out to me with any questions, I would be happy to answer and provide more information.

My motivation

With a strong foundation in problem-solving, communication, and teamwork from my years as a teacher, I am confident that I can bring a unique perspective and set of skills to the field of programming. I am driven by my desire to continue learning, and to use technology to create solutions that improve people's lives.

I am excited to embark on this new journey and to see what the future holds. I am confident that my experience, passion, and dedication will allow me to succeed and make a meaningful contribution to the tech community
